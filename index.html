Be a part of something BIG!

WTP Flag Football is the premier youth football program providing young players an exciting opportunity to engage in a popular non-contact, five-on-five game filled with fun and action while learning lessons in teamwork.

  • Make it Fun
  • Limit Standing Around
  • Everyone Plays
  • Emphasize the Fundamentals
  • Teach Every Position to Every Participant
  • Incorporate a Progression of Skill Development for Every Participant
  • Yell Encouragement, Whisper Constructive Criticism
