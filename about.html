ABOUT OUR PROGRAM

NFL Flag Football is the premier youth football program providing young players an exciting opportunity to engage in a popular non-contact, five-on-fivegame filled with fun and action while learning lessons in teamwork

WTP Flag Football provides an NFL Flag program for boys and girls ages 9-14 who are residents of the Warren Hills school district in Warren County NJ

We have separate Boys and Girls divisions (not coed) with 2 age groups for each; 9-11 & 12-14 years old. Age eligibility is determined based on how old player will be on September 1st, 2017


OUR GUIDING PRINCIPALS

  1. Make it Fun
  2. Limit Standing Around
  3. Everyone Plays
  4. Emphasize the Fundamentals
  5. Teach Every Position to Every Participant
  6. Incorporate a Progression of Skill Development for Every Participant
  7. Yell Encouragement, Whisper Constructive Criticism

LEAGUE OVERVIEW

  • Season will operate from April - June
  • Available to residents of Warren Hills sending district only
  • Divisions; Boys 9-11, Boys 12-14, and Girls 9-14
  • Minimal time commitment required
  • Games scheduled once a week on Sunday
  • Very limited practice time
  • Team size will be 7-10 players, based on response
  • Teams coached by Warren Hills Blue Streaks athletes and alumni
  • 5 players required to play a game
  • Designed to have no confliction with other sports (baseball, lacrosse, etc.)
  • Footballs, flags, & game equipment will be provided
  • Players must wear a mouthpiece (not provided)
  • Wearing soft cleats is encouraged, but not required
  • Cost per player will be $74 (multi sibling discount)
  • Cost includes an official reversible NFL Flag team jersey for each player to keep
