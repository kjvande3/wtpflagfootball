All participants MUST register online per NFL Flag requirements; in order to participate, you need to successfully complete the registration steps within your profile below



PLAYER REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1: Player Participation Form

***This short form is required in order to participate as a player in any NFL Flag Football program. This makes you eligible for our league insurance. So be sure to properly identify our league. Please visit this link, and follow the instructions below...***

  1. State = New Jersey
  2. League = WTP Flag Football (Warren Hills School District)
  3. Fill out Parent and Player information
  4. Read & agree to the terms and conditions
  5. Initial and press SUBMIT
  6. Be sure to check your E-mail for a confirmation notice

Step 2: WTP Flag Football League Registration here

***This link is to register a player to the WTP NFL Flag League. It also provides us basic player & parent/guardian information and sets you up in our league system. Please visit this link, and follow the instructions below...***

  1. Click the button CLICK HERE TO REGISTER NOW
  2. Select 2017 Player Registration (2017 Spring Season) | Register Now
  3. LOGIN with your USA Football account or CREATE a new member account (no fees involved)
  4. Once logged in, either select your name if listed, or add new registrant
  5. Select "Register now" or "Continue to Registrant Information"
  6. Complete Guardian, Player & select Payment Option, then "Confirm Registration"
  7. You should see a message that says "ALL DONE" and you will receive a confirmation email

COACH REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1: NFL Coach Agreement

***This short form is required in order to participate as a coach in any NFL Flag Football program. This makes you eligible for our league insurance. So be sure to properly identify our league. Please visit this link, and follow the instructions below...***

  1. State = New Jersey
  2. League = WTP Flag Football (Warren Hills School District)
  3. Fill out Parent and Player information
  4. Read & agree to the terms and conditions
  5. Initial and press SUBMIT
  6. Be sure to check your E-mail for a confirmation notice

Step 2: WTP Flag Football League Registration here

***This is to submit for registration as a member of the WTP Streaks Coach/Mentor progam and sets you up in our league system. Please visit this link, and follow the instructions below...***

  1. Click the button CLICK HERE TO REGISTER NOW
  2. Select 2017 Coach Registration (2017 Streaks/Coaches) | Register Now
  3. LOGIN with your USA Football account or CREATE a new member account (no fees involved)
  4. Once logged in, either select your name if listed, or add new registrant
  5. Select "Coaches" or "Continue to Registrant Information"
  6. Be sure to include your mobile phone number in the correct field
  7. Press "Continue to Waiver" on the bottom of the page and then "Confirm Registration"
  8. You should see a message that says "ALL DONE" and you will receive a confirmation email
